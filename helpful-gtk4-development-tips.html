flatpak-builder --run --socket=session-bus ./build com.verynoisy.najork.json /usr/bin/gtk4-icon-browser

Not many, huh? Use the systme ones:

env=XDG_DATA_DIRS=/run/host/share:/app/share:/usr/share:/usr/share/runtime/share
